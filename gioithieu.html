ORIGINAL

In 2001, the Marc Jacobs brand began to produce many fragrances
with the first fragrance called Marc Jacobs For Women dedicated to women.
And a year later came the appearance of Marc Jacobs perfume for men.

Achivement

Brand Marc Jacobs has cooperated with many other famous perfume brands in the world.
Up to now, there have been a total of 75 fragrances of various sizes and scents.
In which, there are 54 types of women's perfumes, 16 types of perfumes for both men and women.
